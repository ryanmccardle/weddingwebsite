Bring a plate lunch

The bring a plate lunch starts at midday the day after the wedding (11th of March).

The venue is Outhwaite hall, located in Outhwaite park at the corner of Park Road and Grafton Road (google maps link). Grafton train station is a very short walk away if you want to take the train. There is a limited number of off street parks which we can use, and on a Sunday there should also be parking in the surrounding streets. The hall has a kitchette with a fridge and hot water (no cooking elements), and a toilet.

The park surrounding the hall is lovely so feel free to bring a picnic blanket and hope for sunny weather, but there is also seating inside. There is also a decent playground for the young (or young at heart).
